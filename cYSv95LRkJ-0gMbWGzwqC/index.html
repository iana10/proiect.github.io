Our Story

Happy Beans was created in order to mix the best of both worlds, cats and coffee.

Our beans are sourced from the finest coffee plantations around the world, ensuring a rich and flavorful experience with every sip.

Join us in our cozy space and let the aroma of freshly ground coffee beans awaken your senses while you are surrounded by cute little kitties who are looking for a forever home.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your coffee unique?

Our coffee is meticulously sourced and roasted to perfection, ensuring a delightful and distinctive flavor profile. The coffee is stored at the perfect temperature being monitored by our specialists.

Do you offer dairy-free options?

Yes, we provide a variety of dairy-free alternatives to complement your coffee experience such as oat, soy or rice milk.

Are reservations required?

Reservations are not mandatory, but they are recommended for larger groups and special events.

How easy is the adoption process?

To be sure that our cats will go to a safe home, the adoption process is similar to the one you complete when going to a shelter or a pet shop so we can be able to monitor our recent adopters.

Can I purchase your coffee online?

Yes, you can explore and purchase our signature coffee blends through our online store.
